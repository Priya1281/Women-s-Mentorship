A highly selective 90-day program to build the next generation of women leaders at Plum — with real sponsorship, real community, and real outcomes. 90 DAYS 4 MODULES 10–15 WOMEN · BY APPLICATION 1:1 LEADER SPONSORSHIP Apply now Applications close April 30, 2026 THE PROBLEM Women aren't held back by capability. They're held back by the system. Five structural and social barriers quietly shape who gets to grow — and who doesn't. This program is designed to dismantle every one of them. 01 Limited access to high-visibility opportunities The stretch assignments, the high-stakes projects, the rooms where decisions are made — these don't reach everyone equally. 02 Lower sponsorship in decision-making forums Mentors give advice. Sponsors open doors. Women are over-mentored and under-sponsored. 03 Reduced exposure to leadership networks Access to the right people — and the right conversations — is rarely distributed fairly. 04 Social conditioning that silences ambition Years of being told to be agreeable, not to ask for too much, and to wait your turn makes it genuinely hard to voice what you want — even when you know exactly what that is. 05 The assumption that life milestones are career interruptions Women are still seen through the lens of what might pause them — a wedding, a pregnancy — rather than what they're actively building. This shapes who gets invested in, and who gets passed over. WHAT THIS BUILDS Four outcomes, not four workshops. Decision influence Shape key decisions at the right tables Leadership visibility Be seen and heard where it matters Strategic confidence Lead with clarity and intentionality Sponsorship access Open doors through structured sponsorship HIGH SELECTIVITY Every participant enters with a defined goal. This is not a community program or a celebratory initiative. Selection is application-based and deliberate. Each woman must articulate a clear, specific leadership goal she will work towards over the 90 days — and own it publicly at the closing showcase. Clear stretch goal A defined leadership outcome you are working towards — not a vague aspiration. Manager endorsement Your manager commits to creating space and championing your stretch goal. Full program commitment All four modules, the offsite, and the closing showcase. No partial participation. Cohort of 10–15 Small by design. Every participant is expected to contribute, not just attend. PROGRAM STRUCTURE 90 days · 4 modules · 1 showcase. Each module is led by a woman from a different walk of life — founders, operators, artists, scientists, leaders who've navigated their own version of this. Every module includes one group workshop and dedicated 1:1 time with another woman in the cohort. KICK-OFF Induction & goal-setting Launch event, applications reviewed, cohort announced. Each participant defines and submits her 90-day stretch goal before Module 1 begins. You'll also meet your sponsor for the first time. WEEKS 1–3 · MODULE 01 Understanding the room How power actually moves in organisations — who gets heard, who shapes decisions, and why it often isn't the most qualified person in the room. You'll map the informal power structures around you and figure out how to show up differently in them. 1 workshop 1:1 cohort session Led by a woman from a different field WEEKS 4–6 · MODULE 02 Making your work visible How to get credit for what you do, speak up in rooms that matter, and translate the work you're already doing into language that lands with the people who need to hear it. Because good work that goes unseen doesn't move careers forward. 1 workshop 1:1 cohort session Led by a woman from a different field WEEKS 7–9 · MODULE 03 Learning to say no Protecting your focus, your energy, and your ambitions — without guilt. This isn't about being difficult. It's about being deliberate. You'll practise the conversations that most women have been quietly avoiding, and learn that boundaries are a leadership skill. 1 workshop 1:1 cohort session Led by a woman from a different field WEEKS 10–11 · MODULE 04 Finding your people Who opens doors for you — and how do you actually activate those relationships? This module is about identifying the five people who will shape your next decade, building your personal board of directors, and making your sponsor relationship work for you. 1 workshop 1:1 cohort session Led by a woman from a different field WEEK 12 Closing showcase Each participant presents her stretch outcome to leadership. This is the moment — not a debrief, a debut. THE CORE STRATEGIC LEVER Internal sponsorship, not mentorship. Mentorship provides advice Guidance, perspective, shared experience. Reactive to your questions. Valuable — but not sufficient. • Offers counsel and experience Reactive to your agenda Develops your thinking Stays within your current circles Sponsorship creates opportunity Each participant is paired 1:1 with a senior Plum leader who actively advocates — not just advises. • Visible advocacy in leadership forums High-impact introductions and warm handoffs Strategic navigation of the organisation One door-opening action per cohort member WHAT WE ASK OF SPONSORS ~3 hours total Across the full 90-day program — roughly one hour a month Kick-off call 30 mins to meet your participant and understand her stretch goal Midpoint check-in 45 mins at Week 6 to review progress and unblock where needed One active act An introduction, a nomination, or a visibility opportunity — something real Closing showcase Show up and be seen cheering her on Speak up in rooms Name her when she's not in the room. That's what sponsorship looks like. COMMUNITY LAYER Women community spaces — beyond the cohort. Before the cohort closes in on itself, we open it outward. DEDICATED TOPICS OF INTEREST Conversations built for women — not retrofitted for them. 01 Navigating imposter syndrome Reframe self-doubt as data — and learn to act despite it. 02 Managing up and across Influence without authority. Build strategic relationships in every direction. 03 Financial literacy & equity negotiation Understand comp structures, negotiate with confidence, close the gap. 04 Work-life integration Not balance — integration. Design a career that sustains you for the long game. 05 Your personal board of directors Identify, cultivate, and activate the five people who will shape your next decade. WOMEN OFFSITE A day to slow down and go deeper. Leadership is hard. Sustaining it is harder. The offsite is dedicated space — away from Slack, away from sprints — for emotional and mental wellbeing. Not a team-building exercise. A reset. Emotional regulation under pressure Tools for when leadership gets hard and visibility is high Identity & ambition Who are you becoming — and who do you want to be? Peer support structures Building accountability pairs and trust within the cohort Unstructured recovery time Because rest is not the opposite of ambition WHAT WE NEED FROM LEADERSHIP Three asks to make this work. 1 Sponsorship Senior leaders commit to one participant each — advocate actively, make one meaningful introduction, and show up to the closing showcase. 2 Manager support Managers endorse their participant's application, champion their stretch goal, and create real space for leadership growth — not just in theory. 3 Visibility Create one opportunity per participant during the 90 days to present, lead, or influence at a meaningful forum.
